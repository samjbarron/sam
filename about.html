Hello

I’m Samantha Barron, an artist and visual storyteller interested in photography, archives, memory, collage, movement, and theatrical imagery.

My work explores softness, nostalgia, texture, personal collections, and visual environments that feel suspended somewhere between performance, dreamscape, and documentation.

This website acts as a living scrapbook and gallery space for ongoing projects, artwork, photography, and experiments.
